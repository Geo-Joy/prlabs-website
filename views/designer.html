Do you ever wonder what happens inside the cloud?

Based in New York and Cambridge, DigitalOcean is a dynamic, high-growth technology company that serves a robust and passionate community of developers around the world. Our mission is to simplify cloud computing for every developer. We are working on solving some of the most challenging and interesting technology projects around, on a scale unmatched by few companies.

We are looking for a technical leader who will drive engineering excellence across all major components of our current and future Storage offerings. You will have strong ownership over new and existing systems, guiding multiple teams through all phases of software development, including product definition, implementation, operations and scaling. You’ll guide your team of managers as they execute, coaching and directing them to new levels of excellence. Your work will have a direct impact on real DO customers.

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Working in close partnership with Product Management, Design and other core product directors (Compute and Networking) to deliver storage-related software services.

  • Develop quarterly goals and objectives that help keep your teams aligned with the vision and higher-level company goals.

  • Make large prioritization decisions, and tradeoffs between schedule, scope, cost

  • Attract, develop and retain the people required to deliver your plan.

  • Actively coach and develop the managers and technical leaders within your organization, and create a healthy culture that supports delivery over the long term

  • Command the economics tied to your organization. This includes ROI considerations, revenue impact, margins, opex and capex budgets.

  • Actively participate in the yearly and quarterly budget process and make strategic and tactical decisions to manage to these budgets

  • Be an open communicator within DigitalOcean in both written and spoken form. Routinely present at company AMAs.

  • As a director you will be expected to drive a company initiative that does not fit nicely in a box, or has impact beyond your immediate organization

What We'll Expect From You:

  • Significant experience managing software development teams and shipping software-based products or services, a big plus to Storage-related products.

  • Experience with building and leading teams with an emphasis on scaling them to meet the demands of a rapidly growing business

  • Direct experience building scalable infrastructure software and/or distributed systems

  • Strong analytic and quantitative analysis skills

  • Strong communications skills with the ability to prioritize well and understand how to drive a high level of focus and excellence through a strong, talented and motivated team

  • Sound business judgment, with a proven ability to influence others and drive consensus

  • A strong sense of ownership, urgency, and drive

  • A good systems thinker, who understands complex organisations, systems and how to work effectively in a complex environment.

  • Enough architecture and engineering chops to hold your teams to high standards.
